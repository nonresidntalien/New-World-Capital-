# New-World-Capital-
New World Capital
New World Capital is an independent, self-funded investment vehicle operating at the intersection of macroeconomic thesis construction, digital asset allocation, and contrarian capital deployment. Founded with zero external capital, the fund deploys across five mandates: macro and geopolitical theses, on-chain digital assets, commodities and hard asset preservation, short selling and forensic analysis, and the exploitation of reflexive feedback loops in both macro and micro economies.

Every position held and every thesis published is a timestamped record of conviction in motion. The fund takes long positions on structural transitions — energy infrastructure, digital gold, on-chain savings — and short positions on narrative collapse, where valuations are sustained by political adjacency or manufactured consensus rather than durable economic fundamentals.

New World Capital does not manage external capital and does not provide investment advice. All research is published as a live archive of thesis development — documented before positions mature, not after. The fund exists to study emerging realities early, deploy capital with clarity, and build a sovereign base of financial power at the fault lines of the existing economic order.
